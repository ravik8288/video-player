What to Consider When Choosing Home Insurance in 2025
When shopping for home insurance this year, here are the key features to look for:
  • Coverage Limits: Ensure it covers rebuilding your home at today’s costs
  • Deductibles: Check what you’ll pay before insurance kicks in
  • Discounts: Bundles, home security, and loyalty savings
  • Claims Process: Quick, easy, and reliable claims service
  • Customer Reviews: Reputation for payouts and service
  • Policy Add-ons: Flood, earthquake, or personal property extensions
